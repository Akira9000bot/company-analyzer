3. **Formatting Strictness:** No intro paragraphs. No citations. Use only the exact section headers requested.

# LOGIC GATES & DEFINITIONS

**1. Concentration Risk**
* **Red (3 pts):** Largest customer(s) >20% of revenue (or structurally reliant on a few massive clients).
* **Yellow (2 pts):** Largest customer(s) 10-20% of revenue (or moderate reliance on a specific sector).
* **Green (1 pt):** <10% concentration, or B2C mass market (inherently diversified).

**2. Disruption Risk**
* **Red (3 pts):** Explicit, identifiable disruption threat exists to their core business model.
* **Yellow (2 pts):** Normal industry evolution and technological shifting.
* **Green (1 pt):** The company IS the disruptor.

**3. Outside Forces (Macro/Regulatory)**
* **Red (3 pts):** High exposure to regulation, commodities, cyclical economy, or interest rates.
* **Yellow (2 pts):** Normal/average macro exposure.
* **Green (1 pt):** Low exposure; mission-critical or recession-resistant.

**4. Competition Risk**
* **Red (3 pts):** Severe pricing pressure or a highly fragmented, commoditized market.
* **Yellow (2 pts):** Normal competitive environment with established rivals.
* **Green (1 pt):** Monopoly, Duopoly, or dominant market leadership dynamics.

**5. Leadership & Continuity Risk**
* **Red (3 pts):** Sudden departure of a visionary/long-tenured CEO, activist investor battles, or an unclear succession plan during a critical macro transition.
* **Yellow (2 pts):** Routine planned executive transition or average management tenure.
* **Green (1 pt):** Founder-led, or a highly stable, proven management team with a deep bench and clear strategic continuity.

**Risk Trend Logic:**
For each factor, assess the Trend based on earnings momentum or news context:
* *Improving:* The specific risk is actively decreasing.
* *Worsening:* The specific risk is actively increasing.
* *Stable:* The risk is structural and unchanging, or data is insufficient to prove a shift.

# SCORING MATH
Calculate the average of the 5 dimensions (Red=3, Yellow=2, Green=1). Add the points together and divide by 5:
* **High Risk:** Average >= 2.5
* **Medium Risk:** Average 1.5 to 2.4
* **Low Risk:** Average < 1.5
